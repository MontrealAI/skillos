# SkillOS Autonomous Enterprise Ops Market Proof

This proof is intentionally not an email example.

It is a 100% autonomous proof for a high-value enterprise workflow:

> Procurement invoice reconciliation and payment-risk triage.

## Why this is powerful

The workflow has objective ground truth:

- approve clean invoices
- preserve early-payment discounts
- hold exceptions
- escalate critical payment risks
- block false approvals

## Autonomy constraints

The proof uses:

- no human review
- no emails sent
- no customers contacted
- no private data
- no API keys
- no external services
- deterministic holdout benchmark

## What it proves

It proves that SkillOS can autonomously learn reusable enterprise operations rules from repeated baseline failures and improve holdout performance.

## What it does not prove

It is not audited customer ROI, financial advice, investment advice, or a guarantee of future outcomes.
